Grilled Cheese

Description of the Recipe

This recipe is for a grilled cheese. A classic childhood favorite that even adults love!

Ingredients

  • Cheddar Cheese
  • American Cheese
  • Sourdough Bread
  • Mayonaise
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Get a pan heated to medium on a gas burner.
  2. Take your sourdough bread and place mayonaise on both sides of the bread.
  3. Add your cheddar cheese and american cheese on the inside of your bread.
  4. Place sandwich on pan, wait for cheese to melt and for bread to be toasted on both sides.
  5. Take off pan once brown and melted.
  6. Cut your sandwich in half, and enjoy!
