Scrambled Eggs

Description of the Recipe

This recipe is for a plate of scrambled eggs. Simple, easy, and tastes great!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Black Pepper
  • Pink Salt
  • Parmesan
  • Parsley
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Heat up skillet to medium on a gas burner. Add olive oil to cover the bottom of the skillet.
  2. Crack 3 eggs into a bowl, and whisk it up.
  3. Add eggs to skillet.
  4. Cook until fluffy, make sure the eggs are not brown and dry. Take out eggs and place on plate.
  5. Cut some parsley into small pieces.
  6. Add your salt, black pepper, parsley, and shredded parmesan.
  7. Serve it up and enjoy!
