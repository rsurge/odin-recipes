Lasagna

Description of the Recipe

This recipe is for a lasagna. Typically the dish of choice by our favorite cat, Garfield. It has a stack of pasta noodles, red sauce, and cheese. The cheese can be either ricotta and/or mozzarella.

Ingredients

  • Pasta Noodles
  • Red Sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Parmesan
  • Parsley
  • Lemon Zest

Steps

  1. Boil pasta noodles until slightly bendable.
  2. Heat up red sauce in a saucepan.
  3. Get a baking dish, and start stacking your pasta noodles on a bed of red sauce. Follow this by putting the cheese on top of the pasta noodles. Lastly, pour a layer of red sauce on top of the cheese.
  4. Repeat step three until the baking dish is filled to the top.
  5. Bake the lasagna at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 45 minutes.
  6. Take some sprigs of parsley and take the zest of one lemon as a topping.
  7. Cut into the lasagna, take a piece, and enjoy!
